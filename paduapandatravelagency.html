Travel to The Florida Keys!

With the Padua Panda Travel Agency

The Florida Keys are a chain of islands off the southern coast of Florida. Some of the most popular islands of the Keys are Key Largo, Marathon, and Key West. All make for great family-friendly vacation destinations. They offer a range of activities and events, all of which are sure to keep you intrigued. Even more so, there are numerous relaxation oppurtunities in the Keys. Whether you want a simple, laid-back trip away from the stresses of your daily life or a thrilling tropical adventure, the Florida Keys have it all! There are so many different activities to do and sights to see that you'll never get bored!

The Florida Keys offer outdoor activities like going to the beach, scuba diving, kayaking, and fishing. On rainy days, there are numerous museums, shops, restaurants, and performances to visit.
